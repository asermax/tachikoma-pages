The Dog Pack

Meet Agustin's three companions

Three dogs, three personalities, endless love. Here's the pack that keeps Agustin company.

Taro Youngest

The baby of the family and the most playful of them all. Taro loves playing with other dogs and is friendly with everyone he meets. Always ready for a game!

Maggie Oldest

The senior lady with a big appetite! Maggie is really tame and gentle, but she knows how to be pesky when she wants attention. A true character.

Kuro

A bit older with a BIG personality. Kuro sometimes gets into scuffles with the others, but he's the one who pays Agustin the most attention. Loyal and spirited!
